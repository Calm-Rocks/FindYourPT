## 2. What this policy covers

SpotMyPT is a directory that helps people find personal trainers ("PTs", "trainers") in their area, filtered by specialism, location, and distance. This policy explains what personal data we collect from **both trainers and clients** who use the site, why we collect it, how long we keep it, and what rights you have over it.

If you are a trainer, you'll create an account and a public listing. If you are a client searching for a trainer, you can browse and search without an account, and your data is only collected if you choose to send an enquiry to a trainer.

## 3. What personal data we collect

### If you are a trainer (have a SpotMyPT account)

- **Account details**: your email address and a securely hashed password (we never store your password in plain text — this is handled by our authentication provider, Supabase Auth).
- **Listing details**: your display name, bio, base postcode, coverage area/travel radius, the gym you train from (if any), specialisms, session rate, and any website or social media links you choose to add.
- **Profile images**: a profile photo and up to four gallery images, if you choose to upload them.
- **Verification documents**: your personal trainer qualification certificate and proof of public liability insurance, which you upload so we can verify you before your listing appears in client searches. These documents are stored privately and are **not visible to clients, other trainers, or the public** — only to you and to SpotMyPT administrators carrying out the verification review.
- **Enquiries you receive**: when a client contacts you through the site, we store their name, contact details (email or phone, as they choose to provide), and any message they send, so that you can see and respond to it.

### If you are a client (searching for a trainer)

- **Search activity**: your search location (postcode or town name) and filter choices (specialism, distance) are sent to our search system to find matching trainers, but are **not stored** against any personal profile — searching the site does not require an account and we do not track searches back to an individual person.
- **Enquiry details**: if you choose to contact a trainer, we collect the name, email or phone number, and message you provide, so the trainer can respond to you. This information is shared with the specific trainer you contact — it is not shared with any other trainer or made public.

### Automatically collected information

Like most websites, our hosting and infrastructure providers (Netlify for the website, Supabase for the database) may automatically log technical information such as IP addresses and browser type for security and operational purposes. We do not use this information for marketing or to build a profile of individual visitors.

## 8. How we keep your data secure

We take reasonable technical and organisational steps to protect your data, including:

- Encrypting data in transit (HTTPS) across the entire site
- Storing verification documents in a private file storage area that is not publicly accessible, readable only by you and by SpotMyPT administrators
- Restricting database access through row-level security policies, so trainers can only read and edit their own account data, and enquiry details are only visible to the trainer they were sent to
- Validating uploaded files (profile photos, gallery images, verification documents) to confirm they are genuine image or document files before storing them

No method of storing or transmitting data is 100% secure, but we work to apply appropriate safeguards proportionate to the sensitivity of the data involved.
